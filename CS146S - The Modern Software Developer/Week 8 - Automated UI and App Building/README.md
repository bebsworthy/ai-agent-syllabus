# Week 8: Automated UI and App Building

**Dates:** Mon 11/10 & Fri 11/14

---

## Objectives

Discover how AI democratizes frontend and UI development — enabling anyone to prototype, build, and iterate on full-stack applications with natural language. Explore the new generation of tools that generate complete app scaffolds from a single prompt. Guest: Gaspar Garcia, Head of AI Research at Vercel.

---

## Topics

- Design and frontend for everyone — lowering the barrier to UI/UX creation
- Rapid UI/UX prototyping and iteration — from prompt to deployed app

---

## Reading Materials

No required reading assigned for this week. Focus is on hands-on building.
