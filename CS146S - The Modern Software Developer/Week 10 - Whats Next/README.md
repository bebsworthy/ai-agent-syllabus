# Week 10: What's Next for AI Software Engineering

**Dates:** Mon 12/1 & Fri 12/5

---

## Objectives

Step back and look at the big picture — where is AI software engineering headed over the next decade? Explore the future of software development roles, the emerging paradigms that will define the field, and the industry trends that practitioners should track. Guest: Martin Casado, General Partner at a16z.

---

## Topics

- Future of software development roles — what human engineers will and won't do in 10 years
- Emerging AI coding paradigms — beyond today's agentic tools
- Industry trends and predictions — where the smart money is pointing
